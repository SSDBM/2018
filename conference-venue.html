30th International Conference on Scientific and Statistical Database Management
Bolzano-Bozen, Italy
July 9 - 11, 2018

Conference venue


The SSDBM 2018 Conference will take place at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. For directions and more information about the local area please explore the map below.

Free University of Bozen-Bolzano
Universitätsplatz 1 - piazza Università, 1
39100, Bozen-Bolzano
Italy

How to reach Bozen-Bolzano

Below there is a list of airports close to Bozen-Bolzano with the distance to Bozen-Bolzano and the approximate time to reach the city using public transport, i.e., train or bus:

  • Innsbruck Airport [INN] (121km - ~2.5hours)
  • Verona Villafranca Airport [VRN] (152km - ~2.5hours)
  • Bergamo Orio al Serio International Airport [BGY] (236km - ~4hours)
  • Bologna Guglielmo Marconi Airport [BLQ] (277km - ~4.5hours)
  • Venice Marco Polo Airport [VCE] (273km - ~4.5hours)
  • Milan Linate Airport [LIN] (279km - ~4.5hours)
  • Munich International Airport [MUC] (316km - ~5hours)
  • Milan Malpensa Airport [MXP] (322km - ~5hours)

Directions from INN:
The first option is to take a direct bus from the airport to Bolzano. You can find the timetable of the bus here. The second option is to take the city bus to the Innsbruck railway station and then take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the websites of OBB and Trenitalia.

Directions from VRN:
The first option is to take a direct bus from the airport to Bolzano. You can find the timetable of the bus here. The second option is to take the Aerobus to the Verona Porta Nuova railway station and then take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the website of Trenitalia.

Directions from BGY:
The easiest way is to take a direct bus from the airport to Bolzano. You can use either AltoAdigeBus or Flixbus. The second option is to take the bus to the Bergamo railway station and then take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the website of Trenitalia. Another option is to take the Autostradale bus to Brescia and take the train to Bolzano from there.

Directions from BLQ:
Take the Aerobus to the Bologna Centrale railway station and then take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the website of Trenitalia.

Directions from VCE:
Take the bus to Venezia Mestre railway station and the take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the website of Trenitalia.

Directions from LIN and MXP:
Take one of the buses to the Milano Centrale railway station and then take the train to Bolzano. You can find information about the timetable of the trains on the website of Trenitalia.

Directions from MUC:
The easiest way is to take a direct bus from the airport to Bolzano. You can use either AltoAdigeBus or Flixbus. The second option is to take the S-Bahn to München Hbf and then take either Flixbus or the train to Bolzano. You can find the timetable of the trains on the websites of OBB, Trenitalia and DB.
