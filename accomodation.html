Accommodation


We have negotiated some special discounts at a number of partner hotels for SSDBM participants. In order to take advantage of these special rates, please mention the code 'SSDBM 2018' when you make your booking by phone or by email.

We have pre-booked a certain number of rooms at Hotel Scala, Hotel Luna, Hotel Post Gries, Hotel Magdalenerhof (booking option expires on 20/05/2018). The code 'SSDBM 2018' will grant you access to these rooms.

Important: Bolzano is a touristic town and July is one of the busiest tourist months, therefore, we strongly advise you to make your hotel booking as soon as possible.
